## Description
Annual total of active DACA (Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals) recipients,
2013-2022. Used to calibrate the DACA population projection.

## Source
U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS). "Approximate Active DACA
Recipients." Published quarterly as part of DACA Performance Data.
https://www.uscis.gov/tools/reports-and-studies/immigration-and-citizenship-data

### Key Events
- 2012: Program announced June 15; first applications accepted August 15
- 2013: 472K active recipients (first full year of grants)
- 2017: 800K peak (program suspended for new applicants September 5)
- 2018-2022: Decline due to non-renewal, aging out, and no new initial grants

## Methodology Notes
- Values are approximate end-of-year (December) active recipient counts
- "Active" means valid DACA status (approved initial or renewal)
- Excludes expired, terminated, or pending applications
- 2017 suspension affects new applications only; renewals continue

## Format
- `year`: Calendar year (December stock)
- `total_active`: Approximate number of active DACA recipients
- `source`: USCIS report citation
