## Description
Estimated unauthorized immigrant population residing in the United States, in millions, as of January of each year.

## Sources

All values transcribed from official DHS Office of Homeland Security Statistics (OHSS) reports. DHS publishes only PDF reports; no downloadable data files are available.

### Reports by Coverage Period
- **1990**: INS Statistical Yearbook 1996 (historical estimate)
- **2000-2004**: Hoefer, Rytina, Campbell (2006). "Estimates for January 2005." DHS Office of Immigration Statistics.
- **2005-2007**: Hoefer, Rytina, Baker (2008). "Estimates for January 2007." DHS Office of Immigration Statistics.
- **2008-2009**: Hoefer, Rytina, Baker (2010). "Estimates for January 2009."
- **2010**: Hoefer, Rytina, Baker (2011). "Estimates for January 2010."
- **2011**: Hoefer, Rytina, Baker (2012). "Estimates for January 2011."
- **2012**: Baker, Rytina (2013). "Estimates for January 2012."
- **2013**: Baker, Rytina (2014). "Estimates for January 2013."
- **2014**: Baker (2017). "Estimates for January 2014."
- **2015-2017**: Baker (2021). "Estimates January 2015-2018."
- **2018-2022**: Baker & Warren (2024). "Estimates January 2018-2022." https://ohss.dhs.gov/topics/immigration/unauthorized/population-estimates

## Methodology
DHS uses a "residual methodology": Unauthorized = Total Foreign-Born (ACS) - Legal Foreign-Born (DHS administrative records).

## Format
- `year`: Calendar year (as of January)
- `unauthorized_millions`: Estimated unauthorized population in millions
- `source_report`: DHS report citation
