## Description
Decennial census populations for U.S. territories, used for the TERR component of Social Security area population. Corresponds to TR2025 Input #22.

## Territories and SS Area Start Years
- Puerto Rico (PR): 1951
- Virgin Islands (VI): 1951
- Guam (GU): 1951
- American Samoa (AS): 1961
- Northern Mariana Islands (MP): 1978

## Sources
- U.S. Census Bureau. (1952). 1950 Census of Population, Volume I, Chapter 57: American Samoa, Canal Zone, Guam, Virgin Islands.
- U.S. Census Bureau. (1961). 1960 Census Supplementary Report PC-S1-14: Population Counts for Guam, Virgin Islands, American Samoa, and Canal Zone.
- U.S. Census Bureau. (1961). 1960 Census Supplementary Report PC-S1-15: Population Counts for Puerto Rico.
- U.S. Census Bureau. (Various years). Statistical Abstract of the United States, Section 29: Puerto Rico and the Island Areas.
- U.S. Census Bureau. 2010 and 2020 Decennial Census / Island Areas Census.

## Format
- `census_year`: Decennial census year
- `territory`: Territory code (PR, VI, GU, AS, MP)
- `population`: Total population from decennial census
- `ss_area_start`: Year territory was added to Social Security area
- `source`: Census publication
