## Description
Total civilian population in Alaska and Hawaii for 1940-1949, before these territories became states (1959). Corresponds to TR2025 Inputs #30 and #31.

## Sources
- U.S. Census Bureau. (1943). Sixteenth Census of the United States: 1940. Population, Second Series: Alaska and Hawaii.
- U.S. Census Bureau. (1952). 1950 Census of Population, Volume I: Parts 51 (Alaska) and 54 (Hawaii).
- U.S. Census Bureau. Statistical Abstract of the United States, 1950-1959.

## Notes
- 1940 and 1950 are decennial census counts
- Intercensal years (1941-1949) are linear interpolations between 1940 and 1950 census values
- Alaska: 1940 Census = 72,524; 1950 Census = 128,643
- Hawaii: 1940 Census = 423,330; 1950 Census = 499,794

## Format
- `year`: Calendar year
- `territory`: Territory code (AK or HI)
- `civilian_population`: Civilian population (excluding military stationed there)
- `source`: Data provenance
