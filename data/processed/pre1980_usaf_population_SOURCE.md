## Description
Census Bureau intercensal population estimates of U.S. resident population plus Armed Forces overseas, by sex, as of July 1 for 1940-1979. Corresponds to TR2025 Input #7.

## Sources
- U.S. Census Bureau. (1965). Current Population Reports, Series P-25, No. 311: Estimates of the Population of the United States, by Single Years of Age, Color, and Sex: 1900 to 1959. [Covers 1940-1959]
- U.S. Census Bureau. (1974). Current Population Reports, Series P-25, No. 519: Estimates of the Population of the United States, by Age, Sex, and Race: April 1, 1960 to July 1, 1973. [Covers 1960-1969]
- U.S. Census Bureau. (1982). Current Population Reports, Series P-25, No. 917: Preliminary Estimates of the Population of the United States, by Age, Sex, and Race: 1970 to 1981. [Covers 1970-1979]

## Format
- `year`: Calendar year (July 1 estimate)
- `male`: Male population (resident + armed forces overseas)
- `female`: Female population (resident + armed forces overseas)
- `source`: Census Bureau publication series number
