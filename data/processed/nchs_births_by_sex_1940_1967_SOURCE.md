# NCHS Births by Sex, 1940-1967

## Source
CDC NCHS National Vital Statistics Reports, Volume 53, Number 20 (June 2005)
**Table 1:** "Number of male and female births, sex ratio at birth, and number of excess males: United States, 1940-2002"

## URL
https://www.cdc.gov/nchs/data/nvsr/nvsr53/nvsr53_20.pdf

## Notes
- Pre-1972 data based on 50% sample (already adjusted by NCHS in published totals)
- This file covers 1940-1967 only; 1968+ data comes from NBER microdata (nchs_births_by_sex target)
- TR2025 Input Data Item 35 (Section 1.4.b) and Item 32 (Section 1.5.b) specify "births by month and sex of child, for years 1931-2023"
- Values extracted from PDF using pdftotext on 2026-02-12
